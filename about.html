About Our Service

Professional Drone Services in Wigan

Wigan Drone Services is operated by a CAA-certified drone pilot with extensive experience in aerial photography and commercial inspections. Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable drone services to businesses and individuals in the Wigan area.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Wigan Drone Services was born from a passion for aerial photography and a desire to help local businesses and individuals capture unique perspectives of their properties and events. Our operator has been flying drones for over 5 years and holds all necessary certifications to operate commercially.

Equipment & Technology

We use state-of-the-art DJI drones equipped with high-resolution cameras and advanced stabilization systems. Our equipment is regularly maintained and updated to ensure the highest quality results for our clients.

Safety & Compliance

Safety is our top priority. We strictly adhere to CAA regulations and maintain comprehensive insurance coverage. All flights are logged and documented in accordance with legal requirements.
