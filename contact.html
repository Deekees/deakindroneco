Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover?

We primarily serve the Wigan area and surrounding regions. For locations further afield, please contact us to discuss availability and any additional travel costs.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure availability. However, we may be able to accommodate last-minute requests depending on our schedule.

What happens if the weather is bad?

We offer free rescheduling if weather conditions are unsuitable for flying. Your safety and the quality of our work are our top priorities.

Do you offer video editing services?

Yes, we can provide basic video editing services. For more complex editing requirements, we can recommend professional video editors.
